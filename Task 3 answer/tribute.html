Elon Reeve Musk is a businessman and investor. He is the founder, chairman, CEO, and CTO of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO, product architect, and former chairman of Tesla, Inc.; owner, executive chairman, and CTO of X Corp.; founder of the Boring Company and xAI; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; and president of the Musk Foundation.

Timeline of Elon Musk

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1995: Dropped out of Stanford University to pursue entrepreneurial ventures.
  • 2002: Founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.
  • 2004: Became chairman of Tesla, Inc., and played a crucial role in the development of the Tesla Roadster, the first fully electric sports car.
  • 2008: SpaceX's Falcon 1 became the first privately-funded liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit.
  • 2015: Announced the creation of OpenAI, a non-profit artificial intelligence research company.
  • 2020: SpaceX's Crew Dragon spacecraft successfully transported NASA astronauts to the International Space Station, marking the first crewed orbital launch from U.S. soil since 2011.
  • 2022: Tesla unveils the Cybertruck, an all-electric pickup truck.
  • 2024: SpaceX's Starship completes its first manned mission to Mars.
  • 2026: Neuralink achieves major breakthroughs in brain-computer interface technology.
  • 2028: The Boring Company revolutionizes urban transportation with its underground tunnel network.
  • 2030: Elon Musk becomes the first private citizen to travel to Jupiter's moon, Europa, aboard SpaceX's Interplanetary Transport System.
